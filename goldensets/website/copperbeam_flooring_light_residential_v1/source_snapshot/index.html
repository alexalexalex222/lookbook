Our Story

Forty Years Under One Roof

"We still measure a job the way Gary taught us — twice, by hand, before anyone touches a saw."

Copperbeam Flooring started in 1986 as a two-person hardwood refinishing crew working evenings and weekends out of a rented garage near Cheshire Bridge Road. Gary sanded; Pat kept the books and answered the phone that, back then, was bolted to a kitchen wall.

Four decades later the phone number is the same, and so is the standard: no subcontracted install crews, no showroom sample that doesn't match what shows up at your house, and no job considered finished until someone from Copperbeam has walked it with you in sock feet.

Gary & Pat Howington Founders, Copperbeam Flooring
  1. 1986

    Gary and Pat open a single showroom, refinishing hardwood floors evenings and weekends.

  2. 1998

    A dedicated commercial division takes on its first downtown Atlanta office build-out.

  3. 2011

    Luxury vinyl plank joins the lineup once the material finally earns Gary's approval.

  4. Today

    Second-generation leadership, a twelve-person in-house crew, and the same showroom address.
