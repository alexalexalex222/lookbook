What to Expect

Your first visit, considered.

New patients are welcomed the way houseguests are — unhurried, informed, and never handed a clipboard at the door. Here is how the first ninety minutes unfold.

01

A warm welcome

Your paperwork is completed ahead of time, so your visit begins with a tour of the practice and a quiet conversation about your history and hopes.

02

A gentle, thorough exam

Digital imaging, an oral cancer screening, and a careful look at every tooth and the health of your gums — explained as we go, in plain language.

03

A conservative plan

Dr. Caldwell walks you through findings and options, always favoring the least invasive path. Fees are discussed openly before anything is scheduled.

04

Care at your rhythm

We set a recall cadence suited to your health, not a quota — and the same familiar faces greet you at every visit thereafter.

Common Questions

Answers, before you ask

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes. New families are welcomed year-round. We will request your records from your previous practice on your behalf and reserve a full ninety minutes for your first visit.

Do you work with dental insurance?

We file claims with most PPO dental plans and provide a written estimate before any treatment begins, so there are no surprises. Our coordinator is glad to verify your benefits before you visit.

What if I’m anxious about dental visits?

You are in good company — many of our most loyal patients arrived anxious. Appointments are never stacked, noise-dampening headphones are always available, and nitrous oxide can be arranged for treatment visits.

Do you see children?

From the first tooth onward. Early visits are short, gentle, and centered on making the chair feel ordinary — many of our younger patients are the third generation of their family here.

What happens in a dental emergency?

Call us first at 706.555.0143. We hold same-day time in the schedule for urgent needs, and our answering service can reach the on-call clinician after hours.
