The Process

Precision from inspection to installation.

We leave nothing to chance. Our systematic approach to roof replacement guarantees structural integrity, transparent communication, and a flawless final product.

01

Diagnostic Evaluation

We don't guess. We perform a comprehensive exterior envelope inspection, identifying hidden structural vulnerabilities, moisture intrusion, and precise material requirements before drafting your proposal.

02

Material Curation

Select from elite coastal-grade materials. We guide you through architectural profiles, wind-ratings, and color palettes that complement your property's specific aesthetic and neighborhood regulations.

03

Master Craftsmanship

Installation is executed by our dedicated in-house teams—never subbed out to unknown crews. We adhere strictly to Platinum Preferred standards, ensuring every flashing, valley, and ridge is sealed perfectly.

04

Final Quality Audit

A meticulous post-installation inspection. We verify the structural integrity of the build, clean the perimeter completely of all debris and nails, and issue your extended manufacturer warranties.
