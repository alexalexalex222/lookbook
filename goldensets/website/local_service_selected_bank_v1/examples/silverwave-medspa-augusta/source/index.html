Considered Questions

Before You Reserve

Is an in-home visit as clean as a clinic?

Yes. Your provider establishes a sterile field on arrival: sealed single-use instruments, medical-grade disinfection, gloves, and proper sharps disposal — the same standard as a licensed facility.

Who performs my treatment?

A licensed, board-certified provider working under physician medical direction. You will know your provider's name and credentials before we arrive.

Do I need a consultation first?

For injectables, yes — a brief virtual or in-person consultation lets us review your history, medications, and goals before treatment is booked. Skincare and IV visits include an on-arrival assessment.

Is there downtime?

It varies by treatment and by person. Many clients return to their day immediately; some experience temporary redness or swelling. Your provider walks you through aftercare before leaving.

How far will you travel?

Visits within twenty miles of Augusta are included — we regularly serve Evans, Martinez, Grovetown, and North Augusta. Beyond twenty miles, a travel fee applies and is quoted before you book.

What is your cancellation policy?

Appointments may be rescheduled with 24 hours' notice at no charge. Because each visit reserves a dedicated provider, later changes may carry a booking fee.
