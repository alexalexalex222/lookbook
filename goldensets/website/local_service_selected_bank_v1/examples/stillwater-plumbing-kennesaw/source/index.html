Straight Answers

Client questions.

Do you handle emergencies?

Active leaks and failed water heaters jump the queue. We hold same-day slots for existing client-partners and take new emergency calls as capacity allows — phone first, always, so we can talk you through shutting off water at the main.

How do you price your work?

Diagnostics first, then a flat, itemized quote covering materials and labor before any work begins. No hourly ambiguity, and the number we quote is the number you pay unless you change the scope.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — Georgia Master Plumber licensed and fully insured. Where a repair or replacement requires a permit, we pull it and schedule the inspection ourselves rather than leaving that to you.

Tank or tankless water heater?

Honestly: it depends on your gas line capacity, usage patterns, and how long you plan to stay in the home. Tankless wins on longevity and space; a quality tank often wins on installed cost. We size both options against your actual household before recommending either.

Do you warranty your work?

Every repair carries a two-year labor warranty, and we register manufacturer warranties on installed equipment on your behalf. If something we touched fails within the warranty window, the return visit costs you nothing.
