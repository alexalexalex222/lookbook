# Orbital Launch Manifest — Route Prompt

Use for private aerospace, launch-provider, vehicle, propulsion, satellite, and deep-tech engineering websites that need an inspectable technical product narrative rather than generic startup marketing.

Lead with a launch-pad elevation and restrained mission promise. Progress through selectable vehicle assemblies, a capability envelope, orbit visualization, mission sequence, program roadmap, and payload-manifest intake with an editable review state.

Dark editorial atmosphere supports the engineering content; it never replaces it. Keep labels, readouts, diagrams, and forms precise at desktop and mobile widths.

Do not invent vehicle performance, launch dates, certifications, recovery claims, mission history, customers, payload capacity, or pricing. Unsupplied figures stay labeled placeholders or are omitted.
