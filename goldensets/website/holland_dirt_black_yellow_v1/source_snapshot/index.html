How A Job Gets Done

Five Steps. No Surprises.

The same sequence on every job, mountain lot or flat lot, four acres or forty.

  1. 01

    Site Walk & Estimate

    Wyatt walks the property with you, flags problem areas — rock, wet ground, boundary trees — and scopes a written estimate.

    Usually same week
  2. 02

    Utility Locate & Permits

    Underground lines get marked and any required county permits confirmed before equipment moves onto the lot.

    2–5 business days
  3. 03

    Clearing & Haul-Off

    Brush, timber, and stumps cleared to the agreed line. Debris hauled, chipped, or burned per local ordinance.

    1–4 days, by acreage
  4. 04

    Grading To Grade

    Dozer and excavator cut and fill to the finish elevations — on the plan if there is one, on the practical grade if there isn't.

    2–6 days, by terrain
  5. 05

    Walkthrough & Sign-Off

    We walk the finished site together, confirm drainage and grade hold, and close out the job — no loose ends.

    Same day as final grade
