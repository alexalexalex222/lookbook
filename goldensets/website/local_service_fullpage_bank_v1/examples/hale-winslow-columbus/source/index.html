Firm Philosophy

We do not mitigate.
We litigate.

Hale & Winslow LLP operates on a singular premise: maximum pressure yields maximum truth. We accept a severely limited docket of plaintiff cases, ensuring overwhelming resources and absolute focus are brought to bear on every matter we try.

We are not a volume practice. We are a trial firm built for the most consequential disputes in the state of Georgia and across the nation. When opposing counsel sees our name on the docket, they know a settlement will not be reached through exhaustion, but only through adequate compensation for undeniable liability.
