DEPLOYMENT PROTOCOL

THE INSTALLATION STANDARD

01.

DIAGNOSTIC

Thorough structural and material analysis of the existing roof, identifying underlying rot and ventilation failures rather than just providing a visual quote.

02.

PREPARATION

Complete removal to the decking substrate. Remediation of compromised structural elements to ensure a flawless foundation for the new system.
