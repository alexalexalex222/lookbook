# Iron Circuit - Support Donor Notes

## Strong Donations

- Program tabs for any multi-discipline training business.
- Schedule-day selection for classes, sessions, or recurring events.
- First-visit onboarding for intimidating or unfamiliar services.
- Proof-safe staff roles when verified biographies are unavailable.
- Floor-plan and gear-study composition for physical venues.
- Validated trial or consultation intake.

## Keep With The Atom

- Keyboard navigation and ARIA relationships.
- Specific form errors, input preservation, and honest static success copy.
- The beginner/intermediate distinction.
- The high-detail standard for environmental SVG.

## Strip Before Reuse

- Iron Circuit identity and literal copy.
- Schedule times, program labels, floor geometry, coach-role wording, and FAQ
  answers.
- Combat-specific equipment when the recipient is not a training venue.

## Hard Conflicts

Do not combine this full visual system with another full fight-gym anchor. Pick
one anchor, then borrow only section jobs from the other. Never introduce fake
belts, records, championships, testimonials, or stock fighters.
