## Extraction Rules

- Re-author brand, schedule, program, FAQ, and form copy for the target.
- Keep the ARIA wiring, roving focus, specific validation, and reduced-motion
  behavior.
- Treat sample schedules and coach roles as structure, not verified business
  facts.
- Preserve the visual-detail threshold. Do not reduce the training-floor, floor
  plan, or gear study to a few generic icons.
- Map colors onto the target token system instead of copying exact values.
