The Consultation Journey

A blueprint, then a needle.

Nothing at Aurelia begins with treatment. It begins with a plan your Medical Director will actually stand behind.

  1. 01
    45 min In-suite or virtual

    Discovery Consultation

    A full skin and structure assessment with precision mapping, goal intake, and a candid read on what is — and is not — worth treating.

  2. 02
    2–3 days Authored by your Medical Director

    Bespoke Blueprint

    A written protocol sequencing modalities, sessions, and cost — sent to your patient portal before anything is scheduled.

  3. 03
    20–60 min Private treatment suite

    Treatment Session

    Precision delivery under a comfort-first protocol — topical numbing, guided breathing, and a Medical Director on-site for every injectable day.

  4. 04
    24–48 hr check-in Concierge portal

    Recovery & Aftercare

    A same-week check-in, a plain-language aftercare sheet, and a direct line to your care team — not a printed pamphlet.

  5. 05
    Quarterly rhythm Membership-eligible

    Maintenance Rhythm

    A standing cadence built around how your face actually ages, not a generic six-month reminder text.

Before You Book

Questions, answered plainly.

A clinician-led intake: skin and structure assessment, precision mapping, goals discussion, and a candid recommendation. You leave with a written blueprint, not a booked injectable session — treatment is always a separate visit.

Most patients describe injectables as brief pressure rather than pain. Topical numbing is standard on all injectable and laser visits, with additional options available for lower pain tolerance — discussed at consultation, never assumed.

It varies by modality — neuromodulators carry effectively none, RF microneedling runs two to four days of visible texture, and resurfacing can run a full week. Your blueprint states downtime per session so nothing is a surprise on the calendar.

No. Most patients arrive with a general goal, not a modality list. Select “Not sure yet” on the consultation form and your clinician will build the recommendation during your visit.

Yes — third-party financing is available for blueprints over $600, and membership treatment credit can be applied toward any modality. Your patient coordinator will walk through options after your consultation, never before.

Consultations and treatment sessions can be moved with 24 hours’ notice at no charge through your patient portal. Saturday Atelier Hours are limited and non-refundable inside 48 hours due to suite demand.

Every blueprint is authored or countersigned by our Medical Director. Injectables are performed by the Medical Director or a supervised advanced-practice injector; skin renewal and laser services by licensed aestheticians under written protocol.
