# Native Arcade Racer Build Contract

Build a complete playable browser racing game as the first screen. Use a
full-viewport Canvas or proven game-rendering stack appropriate to the target
repository.

Required behavior:

- steering and acceleration;
- visible track boundaries and deterministic collision response;
- ordered checkpoints and lap completion;
- timer, speed, lap, and checkpoint HUD;
- start, pause, resume, victory/finish, and restart states;
- keyboard and touch controls;
- responsive layout at 360px, 390px, and desktop widths;
- no console errors, horizontal overflow, or blank canvas.

Do not add a marketing landing page before gameplay. Do not claim multiplayer,
online services, persistence, procedural generation, or AI opponents unless
they are actually implemented. Use original game identity and geometry rather
than copying the donor name, track, palette, or wording.
