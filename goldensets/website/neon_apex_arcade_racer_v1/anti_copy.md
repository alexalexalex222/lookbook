# Anti-Copy - Neon Apex Arcade Racer

Borrow mechanics and composition, not identity.

Do not copy:

- the Neon Apex name or exact HUD wording;
- the exact cyan, magenta, yellow, and green palette;
- the donor track geometry, car silhouette, timing values, or checkpoint layout;
- literal CSS selectors, tuning constants, or overlay copy.

Do not invent multiplayer, leaderboards, cloud saves, accounts, AI opponents,
or online events. A derived build must use an original game identity, original
track, and mechanics that are actually playable in the browser.
