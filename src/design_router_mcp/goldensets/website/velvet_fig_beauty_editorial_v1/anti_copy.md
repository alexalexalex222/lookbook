# Velvet Fig Beauty Editorial — Anti-Copy

- Do not reuse Velvet Fig, exact headlines, portrait geometry, treatment names, preparation copy, studio names, membership labels, prices, or booking-review language.
- Do not copy the exact rose/cream/green palette, logo mark, illustration paths, filter arrangement, or location data.
- Borrow the portrait-led editorial hierarchy, treatment discovery, preparation sequence, location and membership comparison, and review-before-submit flow only.
- Never invent treatment outcomes, medical or safety claims, locations, availability, credentials, reviews, prices, or membership savings.
- For medspa or clinical work, retain the UX roles while replacing sugaring-specific content and soft editorial cues with the target's verified service model.
