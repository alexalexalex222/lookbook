# Reference Product Black Spec — Anchor Principles

- The hero presents one inspectable watch object as the focal point. Copy stays quiet enough for case, dial, crown, strap, and proportion to carry the first viewport.
- Exploded anatomy connects visible surfaces to actual construction roles; callouts must remain legible and keyboard-independent.
- The process moves from billet and movement through regulation, casing, and certification without pretending unsupplied workshop claims are true.
- Every specification includes measurement context or becomes a labeled placeholder. Decorative numbers are rejected.
- Six surface-detail views use tabs with clear selected state and descriptive panels; mobile keeps the control labels readable.
- Strap selection, included components, price, and reservation review form one compact conversion sequence adjacent to proof.
