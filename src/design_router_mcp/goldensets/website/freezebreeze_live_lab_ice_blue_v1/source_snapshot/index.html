Quick Questions

Will it cool my entire room?

No — and that's the point. FreezeBreeze creates a personal cooling zone just for you. It cools YOUR face, YOUR neck, YOUR side of the bed — without wasting energy on the whole room.

Will it wake my partner?

At under 40dB it's a steady white-noise fan hum. To a sleeping partner it fades into the background — they'll just wonder why you finally stopped tossing and turning.

Does it work in humid climates?

Honest numbers: below ~50% indoor humidity you get the full evaporative effect. At 50–60% it still works (roughly an 8–11°F cooler breeze at typical summer temps). Above ~70%, switch to fan-only — moving air still cools your skin, the mist just stops adding much. That's why there are 3 mist settings: high for dry air, low for muggy days, off for the swampiest nights. And if you run AC or a dehumidifier, your bedroom usually sits at 40–60% humidity even in humid states — indoor air is much drier than outside.

What if it doesn't work for me?

30-day money-back guarantee. Try it for a month. If you're still waking up sweating, send it back. Full refund, no questions. Plus 12-month warranty.
