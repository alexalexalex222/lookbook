Good Questions

Asked before every first visit.

What happens at the first consultation?

You spend a full hour with Maren Ellison, NP — reviewing your history, goals, and any labs. You leave with a written plan and clear pricing. There is no obligation to book treatment that day.

Am I a candidate for medical weight loss?

Candidacy is a medical decision, not a sales one. We screen your history, medications, and lab work first; if a GLP-1 protocol is not safe or suitable for you, we will say so and suggest what is.

How often can I receive IV therapy?

Most members settle into a rhythm of one to two infusions per month, tailored to their bloodwork and season of life. We will never recommend a cadence your labs do not support.

Will injectables look natural?

That is the entire philosophy here. Maren doses conservatively, favors refinement over transformation, and schedules a complimentary two-week review so any adjustment is unhurried and precise.

Is there downtime?

Most treatments allow you to return to your day immediately; injectables may show brief redness or minor bruising. You receive written aftercare guidance before you leave the studio.

How does membership work?

Membership applies a monthly credit toward any treatment and unlocks member pricing across the menu. Details are shared at your consultation — it is an option, never a requirement.
