Your First Visit

An hour that feels considered.

Every new patient visit follows the same unhurried rhythm. Plan for around seventy-five minutes with us.

01

Welcome & Listening

You arrive to a warm greeting and a calm lounge, not a clipboard. We begin with a conversation about your history, your concerns, and how you would like dentistry to feel.

02

Gentle Diagnostics

Low-dose digital imaging, gum health mapping, and a careful bite assessment — narrated as we go, so nothing about your own health feels like a mystery.

03

You leave with a clear, phased care plan and transparent fees. Where treatment can wait, we say so; where it matters, we explain precisely why.

Before You Ask

Questions, answered.

Do you accept dental insurance?

We are insurance-friendly: we file claims on your behalf and give you a written pre-treatment estimate before any work begins. For patients without coverage, our concierge team can walk you through an in-house membership option.

I'm anxious about dental visits. How do you help?

Comfort is engineered into every visit: ultra-quiet handpieces, a comfort menu with aromatherapy and audio, pauses whenever you raise a hand, and sedation options we discuss openly at your consultation. Many of our most loyal patients arrived nervous.

How long is a first appointment?

Reserve about seventy-five minutes. That covers an unhurried conversation, low-dose digital imaging, a gum health assessment, a cleaning when appropriate, and time to review your care plan without feeling rushed.

Do you see children?

Yes — we welcome families and see children from around age three. Early visits are kept short and gentle, and when a specialist is the right call, we coordinate that referral for you.

What if I have a dental emergency?

Call (912) 555-0151 as early in the day as you can. We hold same-day reserve slots for patients of record with pain, swelling, or a broken tooth, and we will always talk you through immediate next steps.
