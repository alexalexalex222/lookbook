## Below-Fold Direction

This business type benefits from a 3-lane service grid (local moves, long-distance, loading help) followed by a straightforward 3-step process section (book → pack → move). The process should feel operational and direct, not poetic.

Below-fold structural guidance:
- Service grid: use the `service_grid_v1` atom with numbered panels. Each panel gets a service name, one-sentence scope, and a "best fit" bottom row.
- Process section: 3 numbered steps with concrete verbs ("Book your date", "We load", "Done by end of day"). No abstract language.
- Trust expansion: star rating, review count, years in service, insurance mention.
- Footer: business name, phone, service area list (Augusta, Martinez, Evans, Grovetown), hours.
