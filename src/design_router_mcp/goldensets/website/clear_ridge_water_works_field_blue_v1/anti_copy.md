# Clear Ridge Water Works - Anti-Copy

This is synthetic visual grammar. Borrow the diagnostic sequence, field-study
composition, and interaction behavior. Do not ship the identity, copy, or
technical drawings one-to-one.

## Do Not Copy

- The Clear Ridge name, mark, exact headline, navigation, section copy, plan
  names, record wording, token values, or diagram geometry.
- Diagnostic readouts as engineering instructions or a verdict about a real
  property.
- The exact well, pressure-switch, filtration, or route drawings.

## Never Invent

- Licenses, certifications, bonded or insured claims, response times,
  guarantees, warranties, prices, discounts, service towns, counties, map pins,
  reviews, ratings, customer names, job counts, measurements, outcomes, awards,
  or years in business.
- Manufacturer approvals or system compatibility claims.
- A successful diagnosis from the symptom selector.

## Asset And Routing Hygiene

- No external fonts, CDNs, analytics, third-party scripts, stock trucks, raster
  filler, data-image blobs, or emoji icons.
- Internal SVG fragment references are allowed for gradients, masks, markers,
  patterns, and clips.
- Do not flatten the page into repeated service cards or repeated house icons.
- Keep the field-service diagnostic spine when borrowing atoms. Proof safety is
  part of the pattern, not optional disclaimer copy.
