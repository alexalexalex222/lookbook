01 Our Philosophy
"Champions are not built by access to equipment. They are built by access to the right people, the right pressure, and the right environment."

EMBERFORGE was built with a single, uncompromising purpose: to train professional combat athletes and produce world-class competitors. We are not a community gym. We are not a fitness center. We do not offer casual memberships, drop-in classes, or hobbyist programming.

Every element of this facility — from the coaches we hire, to the training protocols we deploy, to the fighters we accept — is optimized for one outcome: winning at the highest levels of combat sports.

If you are a professional or serious amateur competitor in MMA, Muay Thai, or Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and you are willing to do the work that produces real results — you belong here. If not, we will kindly redirect you elsewhere.

R.01
Ego is Checked at the Door
Rank means nothing without performance. Every fighter taps and gets tapped.
R.02
Train to Compete, Not to Impress
Everything we do is competition-oriented. No vanity work on the mat.
R.03
Your Teammates Are Your Sharpest Tools
We build a team that makes each other better, not just training partners.
R.04
Recovery Is Part of Training
Sleep, nutrition, and mental health are coached here, not ignored.
R.05
No Tourists
You're building a career, not content. Compete or don't be here.

EMBERFORGE

Our training methodology is built on a four-phase periodization model, adapted from high-level combat sports teams worldwide and refined over 15 years of producing professional fighters.

CAMP STRUCTURE

Foundation & Assessment

We begin every camp with a thorough assessment of the fighter's current technical baseline, physical capacities, and mental readiness. Coaches evaluate movement patterns, reaction timing, and positional tendencies before constructing the individualized training block. No camp starts the same. Every fighter is different.

11 FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

If your question isn't answered here, email us directly at info@emberforgefightgym.example

Can I train here if I've never had a professional fight?
+
Yes — if you have a verified amateur record of 3+ fights, are actively competing, and demonstrate in your trial session that you belong in a professional environment. We evaluate attitude and work ethic as heavily as fight record. Some of our best current members joined before their pro debut.
Do you offer day passes or drop-in sessions?
+
No. The only exception is the approved trial session, which is part of the application process. We do not offer drop-ins, day passes, or casual visits. This policy protects the training environment for our members.
Can I train all three disciplines or just one?
+
All memberships grant access to classes across MMA, Muay Thai, and BJJ. Your primary discipline will guide your individualized program design, but cross-training in all three is encouraged and common — especially for MMA fighters.
What happens if my application is declined?
+
We will tell you exactly why and what you'd need to do to qualify in the future. A declined application is not permanent. We have approved fighters on their second and third attempts after they built the record or addressed the attitude issue that caused the initial denial. We want to help you get here — on the right terms.
Do you take a cut of fight purses?
+
No. EMBERFORGE is a training facility, not a management company. Your fight purse is yours. Our coaches may charge separately for corner work on a per-fight basis (typically included in Pro and Elite memberships), but we never take a percentage of your earnings. We earn through membership, period.
What is your injury and safety policy during sparring?
+
All sparring sessions are supervised by a coach. Sparring intensity is regulated based on where a fighter is in their training cycle. Hard sparring is not permitted within 3 weeks of a fight. All fighters are required to wear appropriate protective gear during contact sessions. We have an in-house physiotherapist and a standing protocol for injury assessment before a fighter can return to full contact.
Do you help with fight booking and promoter relationships?
+
Elite Camp members receive promotional and booking consultation as part of their package. For Pro Fighter and Prospect members, our coaches have extensive promotion relationships and will make introductions and recommendations where appropriate — but formal management is not a service we provide.
