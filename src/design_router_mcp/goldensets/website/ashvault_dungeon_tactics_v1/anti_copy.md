# Anti-Copy - Ashvault Dungeon Tactics

Borrow the mechanics, state hierarchy, and board composition only.

Do not copy:

- the Ashvault name, map layout, unit placement, objective, or event prose;
- the exact ember, blue, red, and green palette;
- literal CSS selectors, turn constants, enemy paths, or board dimensions;
- the donor's symbols or exact status-panel wording.

Do not claim procedural maps, saved campaigns, online play, accounts, or AI
behavior unless those systems exist. A derived build must use an original game
identity and an independently implemented, browser-verifiable rules loop.
