A short path from signal to resolution

The workflow stays legible under pressure.

SignalStack keeps intake, routing, ownership, and review in one operational sequence. Each step leaves a visible record for the next person in the loop.

  1. 01

    Ingest

    Bring in webhooks, log drains, and polling jobs without replacing the systems that already emit the events.

  2. 02

    Correlate

    Shape incoming events into queues and evaluate the rules that determine status, owner, and escalation path.

  3. 03

    Triage

    Claim, snooze, or reassign a signal from the same table where the team sees source context and current state.

  4. 04

    Review

    Export the action trail, inspect the handoff, and tune the rule against what actually happened.

Scoped access

Every role gets a deliberate operating boundary.

Permissions stay readable and reviewable. Operators work the queue, leads shape the rules, and auditors inspect the record without changing it.
